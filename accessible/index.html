Frequently Asked Questions

What are the physical requirements for this trip?

All passengers must pass a comprehensive medical examination to ensure they can handle the rigors of space travel. Generally, you should be in good health with no serious cardiovascular or musculoskeletal conditions. We provide pre-flight training to prepare you for microgravity conditions.

How long does the journey to Neptune take?

With our advanced fusion propulsion systems, the journey from Earth to Neptune takes approximately 3 weeks each way. The total duration of your trip depends on the package you choose, ranging from 7 days to 1 month in the Neptune system.

What safety measures are in place?

Our spacecraft feature redundant life support systems, radiation shielding, and emergency return capabilities. All crew members are extensively trained in emergency procedures. We maintain a 100% safety record across all our missions.

Can I cancel or reschedule my trip?

You may cancel up to 90 days before departure for a full refund. Between 90-30 days, a 50% refund is available. Within 30 days, trips are non-refundable but may be rescheduled (subject to availability) for a 10% fee. We strongly recommend travel insurance.

What should I pack for the journey?

We provide all necessary space suits and safety equipment. You should pack comfortable clothing (we recommend 5-7 outfits), personal hygiene items (all liquids must be in approved containers), and any special medications. A detailed packing list will be provided after booking.
